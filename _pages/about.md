**ZJU100 Young Professor**


## Biography
I am a ZJU100 Young Professor (Principal Investigator) in the College of Software Technology at Zhejiang University (ZJU). I received my Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University in 2025. My research interests lie at the intersection of computer architecture, quantum computing, hardware and software co-design, and electronic design automation (EDA). I mainly focus on microarchitecture design for scalable quantum control systems, FPGA-based pulse generation, and fast quantum feedback. My work has been published in premier computer architecture and EDA venues, including ISCA, MICRO, ICCAD, and TCAD. 

<font color=blue>I am looking for self-motivated Master and PhD students interested in computer architecture and quantum computing. Students with a strong background in FPGA, embedded systems, digital logic, and a passion for hardware-software co-design are especially welcomed. </font>

## Research
**Quantum Computing Control Architecture and Hardware-Software Co-design**
- **Architectural optimization in the peripheral hardware of quantum computer**
  - FPGA-based branch prediction for fast quantum feedback (ARTERY@ISCA’25)
  - Hybrid multiplexing with dynamic qubit grouping for scalable quantum wiring (YOUTIAO@MICRO’25)
- **Electronic Design Automation (EDA) and pulse generation optimization**
  - Fast and precise pulse control with near-quantum cache design on FPGA (SmartQCache@TCAD’25)
  - Accelerating the pulse generation of quantum circuits with reusable patterns (QPulseLib@ICCAD’23)
