**ZJU100 Young Professor**


## Biography
I am a ZJU100 Young Professor (Principal Investigator) at the College of Software Technology, Zhejiang University. My research group is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of computer architecture and hardware-software co-design. Over the past two years, our team has published 4 papers in the "Big Four" premier computer architecture conferences (MICRO, HPCA, ASPLOS, and ISCA)**. 

I am always looking for self-motivated Master's and Ph.D. students, as well as Postdocs, to join our group! 
If you have a solid background in **FPGA or embedded systems**, are familiar with **digital logic**, and have a strong passion for the intersection of computer hardware and software, you are more than welcome to apply. Please feel free to drop me an email with your CV and transcripts.
